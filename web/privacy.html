HydraPal Privacy Policy

HydraPal is designed to help you track hydration with minimal data collection. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and how to contact us.

What we collect

We may store hydration records, target preferences, and app settings on your device. If ads are enabled, the ad provider may collect device and usage signals under its own policies.

How we use data

Data is used to keep your water tracking working, remember your preferences, and improve the app experience.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Some limited information may be processed by service providers needed to run the app.

Your choices

  • You can clear local app data by removing the app or resetting storage on the device.
  • You can contact us if you want help with data questions or account-related concerns.
  • If you do not want ads, please note the app may rely on ad-supported features in some builds.

Children

HydraPal is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will help review it.
